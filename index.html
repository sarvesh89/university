The Cambridge Senior Management Programme is a unique global learning journey over six months, that enables participants to become transformational leaders. Its modular format allows participants to immerse themselves in the inspiring cities of Cambridge and Singapore, engage with 14 world-renowned Cambridge faculty, and interact with an exceptional peer group and alumni network of 9600+ members.

INDIVIDUALISED PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT SESSIONS (IPD)

The IPD will give you a range of opportunities to understand yourself as you look at your behaviour from many different perspectives and use them, along with the other content from the programme, to make significant and sustainable progress on your development plan - for which you are responsible.

Combine class work, small group work, coaching and individual reflection space to maximise your personal learning and development
Pre-work will enable you to start to think about who you are, and how you "show up" in your professional role
Small group and class sessions will provide opportunities to look at yourself and your impact on others during the programme
A 360° instrument gives you the opportunity to get feedback on both you and your impact from those at your regular place of work
A personality framework tool to help you understand the difference between behaviour and preference
Individual coaching over the duration of the programme to make a significant difference to your leadership

PARTICIPANT PROFILE

Participants join us from large organisations, medium-sized companies or even from startups. The Cambridge Senior Management Programme will help senior functional or technical managers make the transition to a general management role, or support those starting new entrepreneurial ventures.

The learning journey is specifically designed for senior managers with:

Accomplished work experience in functional, technical, or business roles

More than 5 years of management experience with a successful track record in one or more functional areas of business

An agenda for change
